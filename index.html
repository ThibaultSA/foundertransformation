A cohort, not a course library. You do this alongside founders carrying the same weight. That's not a nice extra, it's most of the mechanism.

Questions

How much time does it take each week?

Around two to three hours: the lessons, one live call, and ten minutes of daily training.

Is this coaching or a course?

A cohort-based transformation. Lessons, plus live calls, a community, and a 1:1 debrief. You are never watching alone.

What makes it different?

It works on the founder, not the tactics. Character and identity, never hacks.

Do I need experience with this kind of work?

No. Just a real business and a willingness to look honestly at yourself.

What if it turns out not to be for me?

Step out within the first two weeks and you're refunded. No questions.
